Bonjour,

My name is Ali Fakhar (علی فخار). I am a Ph.D. student, focusing on statistics and optimization advised by [Prof.Sophie Achard](https://scholar.google.fr/citations?user=9Xx9B-4AAAAJ&hl=en), Prof.Irene Gannaz and Prof.Kevin Polisano in statisfy group (INRIA), (France, Grenoble, laboratoire jean kuntzmann). My research is supported by a grant from the Multidisciplinary Institute in Artificial Intelligence (MIAI).

My main interests are statistical signal processing, and optimization. I, currently, focus on optimization on the PSD cone and its intersection with chordal graph. The crucial applications of it revolves around correlation matrix and graph learning. We focus on brain and its signal to learn more about brain and correlation of different regions of brain.

I completed my Master's degree in Electrical Engineering with a specialization in Communication Systems at [Sharif University of Technology](https://en.sharif.edu/) in 2023 and [Professor Massoud Babaie-Zadeh](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=5H-SuMcAAAAJ&hl=en) served as my thesis supervisor. I previously obtained a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from IUST in 2021, where Professor Shahrokh Farahmand guided my studies.
