I am Ali, a Doctoral student focusing on Statistics and optimization, more specifically, matrix generation and matrix completion in statistics with the help of high-dimensional sparse convex optimization on the PSD cone in France. I am affiliated with Univ. Grenoble Alpes with collaboration with CNRS and INRIA, under the supervision of Prof. Sophie Achard, Prof. Iren Gannaz, and Prof. Kevin Polisano.

Prior to my PhD, I graduated from the electrical engineering department at Sharif University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Massoud Babaie-Zadeh and IUST in Iran, for my master's and bachelor's degrees, respectively.
