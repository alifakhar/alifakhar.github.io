Arabic: أَفَحَسِبْتُمْ أَنَّمَا خَلَقْنَاكُمْ عَبَثًا (Holy Quran)

Persian: سلطان ازل گنج غم عشق به ما داد* تا روی در این منزل ویرانه نهادیم (حافظ)
English Interpretation: “The Eternal Sovereign gave us the treasure of love's sorrow / When we set foot in this ruined abode. (Hafez)”
Reflection: The Beloved Friend reached behind the veil of possibility and manifested His presence in the veil of appearance (material life).
